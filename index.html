Getting Started

Everything you need to join Eclipse State and start playing.

📥 How to Install FiveM

  • Download FiveMGet the latest version from fivem.net. Make sure you have a legal copy of GTA V installed on your PC.
  • Install FiveMRun the installer and follow the prompts. FiveM will install as a separate application alongside your GTA V.
  • Launch FiveMOpen FiveM from your Start Menu or Desktop. The first launch may take a few minutes to set up.
  • Create or Sign InSign in with your Cfx.re account (required). If you don't have one, create one during the sign-in process.

    • 🔗 How to Join Eclipse State

  • Join Our DiscordFirst, join our Discord server at discord.gg/cU3CH7z494. This is where you'll get support and updates.
  • Open FiveM ConsoleOpen FiveM and press F8 to open the game console.
  • Connect to ServerIn the console, type: connect eclipsestate.com
  • Get Your TokenOnce connected in-game, press F8 again and type token to receive your unique linking code.
  • Link Your AccountCopy the code from in-game, paste it on the Home page of this website, and click "Link Account" to access your dashboard.

    Server Rulebook

    Every player must follow these rules. Breaking rules leads to warnings. 6 warnings = automatic permanent ban. If you have never played FiveM before, please read each rule carefully — the explanations will help you understand what is expected.

    GeneralRoleplayVDM/RDMMetagamingCombatVehiclesEconomyStaffDiscord

    Critical General Conduct

    • Respect everyone at all times.Racism, homophobia, hate speech, sexism, or any form of discrimination or harassment is zero tolerance. You will be permanently banned instantly with no warning and no appeal. This includes voice chat, text chat, and any form of communication.
    • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting.Using mod menus, aimbots, wallhacks, speed hacks, or any third-party tool that gives you an unfair advantage = immediate permanent ban. Exploiting bugs in the game or server scripts (like duplication glitches or teleport exploits) is also a permanent ban. If you find a bug, report it — don't abuse it.
    • Do not impersonate staff or other players.Pretending to be an admin, moderator, owner, or another player to trick or deceive others will get you banned. Staff members will always identify themselves properly.
    • English is the only permitted language.All in-character (IC) and out-of-character (OOC) communication must be in English. This ensures everyone can understand each other and staff can moderate effectively.
    • Staff decisions are final during active situations.If a staff member tells you to do something or makes a ruling in-game, follow it immediately. If you disagree, open a ticket on Discord afterward with evidence. Arguing with staff in-game will result in additional punishment.
    • Record your gameplay.Having video evidence protects you. If someone reports you and you have a recording showing you followed the rules, it can save you from punishment. However, faking or editing evidence to falsely accuse someone = permanent ban.
    • Use common sense.If an action feels wrong or unfair, it probably is. Don't do it. If you're unsure whether something is allowed, ask a staff member before doing it.
    Warning → 3-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

    Critical Roleplay Standards

    • Stay in character (IC) at all times.When you're in the server, you are playing a character. Everything you say and do is part of your character's story. "Fail RP" means breaking character or doing things that make no sense for your character — like dancing during a hostage situation or talking about real-life topics while being robbed. This ruins the experience for everyone.
    • Value your life (Fear RP).If someone points a gun at you, you must roleplay being afraid. Comply with their demands. You cannot suicide to escape a situation, disconnect (combat log), or act like a superhero who isn't scared of death. Your character values their life just like a real person would.
    • New Life Rule (NLR).When your character dies, they forget everything about the events that led to their death. You must stay away from the area where you died for at least 15 minutes. You cannot return to the same situation, cannot seek revenge, and cannot tell others what happened.
    • No powergaming.Powergaming means forcing an action on another player without giving them a fair chance to respond. For example, saying "/me knocks you out" without letting them react is powergaming. Always give other players time to type or speak their response. Roleplay is cooperative — not competitive.
    • Create a realistic character.Your character should be believable. No "secret agent," "military super-soldier," or "billionaire crime lord" backstories without explicit staff approval. Your character should have realistic skills, flaws, and motivations — just like a real person.
    • Robberies must have real roleplay reasoning.You cannot rob someone just because you're bored. There must be an in-character reason for the robbery. Chain-robbing (robbing multiple people back-to-back) or camping popular robbery spots is not allowed. Max 3 robberies per hour per player.
    Warning → 7-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

    Major VDM & RDM — No Random Violence

    • RDM (Random Deathmatch) is forbidden.RDM means killing or attacking another player without any valid roleplay reason. You cannot just walk up to someone and shoot them because you feel like it. Every hostile act must be part of a roleplay story — there must be a reason that makes sense in-character.
    • VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) is forbidden.VDM means running people over with a vehicle or using a car as a weapon. You cannot drive into crowds or use your vehicle to kill people outside of a valid roleplay scenario.
    • Initiation must be clear.Before any attack, the other person must clearly understand they are being threatened or engaged. You can't just open fire silently — there must be verbal or visible clues that a hostile situation is starting. Give them time to understand what's happening.
    • Drive-by shootings require prior escalation.You cannot randomly spray bullets from a car. There must be an ongoing roleplay conflict that leads to the drive-by.
    3-Day Ban → 14-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

    Major Metagaming & Powergaming

    • Metagaming is strictly banned.Metagaming means using information from outside the game (Discord chats, streams, player lists, DMs) to make decisions inside the game. For example, if you see in Discord that someone is at a specific location, you cannot suddenly "find" them in-character. Your character only knows what they've learned in-character.
    • Stream-sniping = permanent ban.If you watch someone's stream to track their location or gain an advantage in-game, you will be permanently banned. No warnings, no appeal. This is one of the most serious offenses on the server.
    • No external communication for IC coordination.Do not use Discord voice calls, party chat, or any external app to coordinate in-character actions unless you are in a staff-approved channel. Your character should communicate through in-game methods.
    • Keep OOC chat out of active RP.The /ooc (out-of-character) command should be used sparingly and never during active roleplay scenes. Breaking character to say something OOC in the middle of a heated RP moment ruins immersion for everyone.
    7-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

    Major Combat Rules

    • Combat must come from genuine escalated roleplay.You cannot start a fight simply because you're bored or want action. There must be a developing story and conflict between characters that naturally leads to combat. Hostility should be the last resort, not the first option.
    • Maximum 6 members per side.In any hostile situation, each group or gang is limited to 6 active members. This keeps fights fair and prevents massive group stomping. If you have a larger event planned, get staff approval first.
    • No camping.You cannot wait outside hospitals, police stations, spawn points, or garage exits to attack people. These are safe zones where players should be able to transition without being ambushed.
    • 30-minute cooldown after combat.After a hostile situation ends (someone dies or both sides disengage), you must wait 30 minutes before re-initiating combat with the same group. This prevents endless revenge loops.
    • No exploiting animations or emotes.You cannot use character animations, emotes, or glitches to gain a combat advantage — such as combat rolling through walls, animation-canceling to shoot faster, or using emotes to glitch through objects.
    3-Day Ban → 14-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

    Minor Vehicle Rules

    • Drive realistically.No speeding through crowded city areas at 120mph. No stunt jumps off every ramp you see. Driving should look believable — your character wouldn't drive like a maniac unless there's a very good roleplay reason (like being chased).
    • Off-roading supercars = Fail RP.Supercars and low-clearance sports cars cannot go off-roading up mountains. It makes no sense and breaks immersion. Use appropriate vehicles (SUVs, trucks, off-road vehicles) for rough terrain.
    • No vehicle exploits.Do not use vehicles to climb buildings, access rooftops, or reach areas that are clearly not meant to be accessible. This includes using ramps, boosting, or any physics tricks to glitch into restricted areas.
    • Aircraft require a pilot's license.If you want to fly planes or helicopters, you must obtain a pilot's license through in-character roleplay. This adds realism and prevents random players from crashing aircraft into the city.
    • Stolen vehicles must be roleplayed properly.If you steal a car, you can't just keep it forever. It must go through IC channels — chop shops, re-plating services, selling to fences, etc. Simply hoarding stolen cars is not allowed.
    Warning → 3-Day Ban → 7-Day Ban

    Minor Economy & Assets

    • No large transfers without approval.Transferring large sums of in-game money between your own characters requires staff approval — this prevents economy manipulation. If you have a legitimate reason, open a ticket and we'll review it.
    • Economy exploits = permanent ban + asset wipe.Duplicating items, abusing money glitches, or exploiting any economy loophole will result in your entire account being wiped (all money, cars, properties removed) and a permanent ban. Your inventory and bank records are logged.
    • Businesses must be actively roleplayed.If you own a business but never open it or roleplay there, it will be revoked and given to someone who will actually use it. "Ghost" businesses hurt the server economy.
    • One residence per character.Property hoarding is not allowed. Each character may own one primary residence. This ensures there are enough houses and apartments available for new players.
    Warning → Asset Wipe → Permanent Ban

    Major Staff & Appeals

    • Staff decisions are final during situations.When a staff member makes a ruling during an active scene, follow it immediately — even if you disagree. You can appeal later through the proper channels. Arguing or being disrespectful mid-situation will result in further punishment.
    • Never ask staff for special treatment.Do not ask staff for free money, items, vehicles, or any in-game advantage. Staff are here to moderate and help — not to give handouts. Asking for favors undermines the integrity of the server.
    • False reports = punishment for you.Filing fake reports against other players to get them in trouble will result in you being punished instead. Only submit genuine reports with real evidence.
    • Staff are community members too.Harassing, stalking, or targeting staff members — in or out of character — is a serious offense and will result in a severe ban. Staff volunteer their time to make the server better. Treat them with respect.
    • Appeals go through Discord tickets.If you believe you were unfairly punished, open a ticket on Discord with your evidence. Be calm, respectful, and factual. Raging or demanding will not help your case.
    3-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

    Minor Discord Rules

    • Follow Discord's Terms of Service.Any violation of Discord's ToS (including underage use, sharing illegal content, or harassment) will result in a ban from our server and a report to Discord.
    • No spamming or flooding.Do not send repeated messages, ghost ping (@mention then delete), or flood any channel with nonsense. This includes DM spamming other community members.
    • Use channels for their intended purpose.Memes go in #media, gameplay clips in #clips, support requests go through the ticket system. Keeping channels organized helps everyone find what they need.
    • Voice channels: be respectful.No ear-rape (extremely loud or distorted audio), disruptive soundboards, or screaming in voice channels. If someone asks you to stop, stop. If staff asks you to stop, comply immediately.
    • Ban evading = permanent community ban.If you are banned and create a new account to rejoin, that is ban evasion. All your accounts will be banned permanently across the entire Eclipse State community — FiveM server and Discord.
    Mute → Kick → Ban
